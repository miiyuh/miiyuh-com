# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Anyone visiting the portfolio — potential clients, collaborators, employers, or just curious visitors browsing photography, artwork, projects, and blog content.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio showcase for miiyuh's creative work (photography, artwork, projects, blog). The optional surveys feature exists as a self-hosted alternative to Google Forms, should it ever be needed — it's not a primary surface.

## Brand Personality

Simple, data-driven, sharp.

## Anti-references

Anything that looks AI-generated or leans into corporate-bullshit aesthetics. No template-driven layouts, no stock-photo vibes, no generic SaaS gloss. Every design choice should feel intentional and human.

## Design Principles

1. **The site is its own portfolio piece** — the design and execution should themselves demonstrate the quality of work. No half-measures.
2. **Let the work breathe** — photography, artwork, and projects are the stars. Layout, spacing, and typography serve the content, not compete with it.
3. **Sharp but warm** — data-driven clarity in structure and type, with personality in texture, motion, and voice. Never cold, never corporate.
4. **Nothing AI-generic** — every component, layout, and interaction should feel hand-crafted. If it looks like a template, cut it.
5. **Inclusive by default** — WCAG AA, `prefers-reduced-motion` support, responsive across all viewports.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as a baseline, reduced motion support via `prefers-reduced-motion`, responsive design across all viewports. Open to additional recommendations.
